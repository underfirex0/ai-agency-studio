Every commission begins with a refusal to design. Before a plan is drawn, we spend time understanding what went wrong the last time — and why the original brief contained the answer, if only someone had listened carefully enough to find it.

01

Material decision · March 2024

The threshold step was widened by 40mm.

The original specification called for a flush threshold. Site survey revealed a 3mm seasonal movement in the adjacent masonry. We widened the step to allow for this without telegraphing the fix — the threshold now reads as a deliberate proportional choice, not a correction.

02

Material selection · January 2024

The first limestone sample was declined after three site visits.

The specified Jura Beige showed acceptable laboratory results but read as warm under the project's north-facing glazing. We sourced a grey limestone from the same quarry with a 14% higher silica content — cooler, closer to the light the building actually receives.
